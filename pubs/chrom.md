## Abstract

We prove that for sufficiently large $K$, it is NP-hard to color 
$K$-colorable graphs with less than $2^{\Omega(K^{1/3})}$ colors. 
This improves the previous result of $K$ versus $K^{\frac{1}{25} \log K}$
by Khot.
